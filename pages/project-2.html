LinkedIn Jobs Digest Bot

Automated pipeline that collects new IT job postings from LinkedIn, normalizes the data, and publishes concise summaries on a schedule.

Project Overview

This project reduces the noise of daily job boards by aggregating relevant LinkedIn postings and transforming them into short, consistent updates.

The workflow includes filtering for IT roles, deduplication, and scheduled publishing, making it easier for early-career candidates to track opportunities.
